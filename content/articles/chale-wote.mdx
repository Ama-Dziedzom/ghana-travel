Chale Wote Street Art Festival brings together thousands of art lovers from across the globe. The name "Chale Wote" literally means "Friend/Buddy, let's go" in Ga, and it perfectly captures the communal spirit of the event.

From massive murals to avant-garde performance art, the festival is a testament to Accra's position as a rising global hub for creativity.
