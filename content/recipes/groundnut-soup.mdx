---
title: "Creamy Groundnut Soup"
slug: "groundnut-soup"
category: "soups"
description: "A rich, nutty, and spicy soup made from roasted peanuts, usually served with fufu or rice balls (omtuo)."
coverImage: "/images/recipe_groundnut_soup.png"
prepTime: 15
cookTime: 45
servings: 4
difficulty: "medium"
ingredients:
  - "1 cup natural peanut butter (no added sugar)"
  - "500g chicken or goat meat, cut into pieces"
  - "2 large tomatoes"
  - "1 large onion"
  - "Fresh ginger and garlic"
  - "2 Scotch bonnet peppers"
  - "1 tbsp tomato paste"
  - "Salt and bouillon cubes to taste"
instructions:
  - "Season the meat with salt, blended ginger, garlic, and onions. Steam in a pot for 10-15 minutes."
  - "In a separate pan, mix the peanut butter with a little water and the tomato paste. Stir over low heat until the oil begins to surface."
  - "Add the peanut mixture to the steaming meat."
  - "Add whole tomatoes, onion, and peppers to the pot. Once they are soft, remove them, blend, and strain back into the soup."
  - "Add enough water to reach your desired consistency and simmer for 25-30 minutes until the oil rises to the top."
tips: "The oil rising to the top is the sign that the soup is ready. Serve with fufu for the most authentic experience, but it's also delicious with plain steamed rice."
---

Groundnut soup is the ultimate comfort food. It's savory, slightly sweet from the peanuts, and has just enough kick to warm you up.
